About Indepth Precision Consult LTD

Indepth Precision Consult LTD is a firm of experienced Survey Sampling Statisticians, Public Health Specialists, Demographers, Computer Scientists and GIS experts with a mission to transfer skills and excel in the delivery of high quality services using modern techniques.

Our History

Established in 2004, Indepth Precision Consult LTD was formed by a group of core management and technical staff who resolved to pull together their expertise in data generation, analysis, computerization, and dissemination. Our aim was to provide greater efficiency and effectiveness in delivering valuable information for valid conclusion and decision-making.

Our Assets

  • Highly skilled team with diverse expertise
  • Powerful computing systems and statistical software
  • Comprehensive database of Nigerian localities
  • Extensive field work experience across Nigeria

Our Approach

  • Combining the best people with quality training
  • Utilizing exceptional technical resources
  • Applying management expertise to research services
  • Prioritizing client interests in all our work

Our Structure

Our flexible and robust management structure ensures prompt service delivery, effective coordination, innovation, and knowledge sharing across our nationwide operations.

  • Principal Consultant/Chief Executive Officer
  • Senior Consultants leading specialized divisions
  • Technical Advisory Board composed of leading professionals and academics
  • Zonal Managers coordinating activities across Nigeria's geopolitical zones
  • State Coordinators overseeing implementation at the local level
